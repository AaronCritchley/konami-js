Touch Gesture

Swipe your thumb (not the screen), up, up, down, down, left, right, left, right, tap, tap, tap.

Note: "up" means you drag your thumb from the botton to the top (the screen moves down) while "left" means that you swipe from right to left (the screen moves right).
